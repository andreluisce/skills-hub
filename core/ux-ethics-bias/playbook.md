# Playbook

## Sources
- Design for Cognitive Bias — David Dylan Thomas
- Laws of UX — Jon Yablonski

## Objective
Usar psicologia for orientar and simplificar — nunca for manipular and prender.

## Regra Central
Responsible design leverages biases to simplify.
Dark pattern explora biases for manipular.

## Biases to Watch
- Framing Effect: enquadramento informa or distorce?
- Loss Aversion: alarmist copy to force action?
- Scarcity: escassez informativa or fabricada?
- Cognitive Fluency: visual facility does not validate false claims.
- Peak-End: critical error at the end destroys accumulated positive memory.

## Positive Friction — Quando Usar
Irreversible, financially sensitive, privacy, legally relevant decision.
Includes: clear confirmation actions, consequence summary, undo option.

## Dark Pattern Red Flags
- Ambiguous or misleading main button.
- Cancelamento oculto without necessidade.
- Pre-booked opt-in for recurring billing.
- False urgency without real basis.
- Bloqueio artificial for exportar/apagar data.

## Review Protocol
1. Does the user understand what they are consenting to?
2. Is there a way to refuse without punishment?
3. Consequences explained in simple language?
4. Design protects vulnerable users?
