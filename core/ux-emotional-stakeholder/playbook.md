# Playbook

## Sources
- Designing for Emotion — Aarron Walter
- Articulating Design Decisions — Tom Greever

## Objective
Create emotionally relevant experiences and defend every decision with objective evidence.

## Modelo Emocional
1. Visceral: first impression, visual identity, tone.
2. Behavioral: utilidade, previsibilidade, confiabilidade.
3. Reflective: memory, meaning, reputation of the experience.

## Model of Communication
Toda proposta explica:
1. Que problema resolve.
2. For those who solve it.
3. Qual metric melhora.
4. Qual trade-off assume.
5. Qual plan of validation confirma.

## By Audience
- Executives: risk, return, strategic priority.
- Engenharia: viabilidade, complexidade, estados of error.
- Produto/PM: escopo, sequencing, impacto incremental.
- Marketing: consistency of narrative, perception of confidence.

## Anti-Patterns
- "It got better" without criteria.
- Purely aesthetic defense.
- Present only one option without baseline.
